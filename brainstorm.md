## Problem
College students struggle with:
- Overspending on food due to lack of planning
- Eating unhealthy meals because of limited time & knowledge
- Not knowing how to combine budgeting and nutrition

## Solution
PlatePilot helps students plan weekly meals efficiently, track nutrition and calories, stay within a budget, and organize grocery shopping. It recommends affordable, healthy recipes based on dietary preferences, time available, and remaining budget.

## Target Audience
- College students and young adults who cook for themselves
- People trying to save money and eat healthier

## Core Features (MVP)
- Meal Feed using MealDB API (TableView)
- Meal Detail Screen (ingredients, calories, price estimate)
- Add meal to weekly plan
- Grocery list generator
- Track weekly spending & calories
- Favorites (save meals locally)

## Stretch Features (optional)
- Search / filtering
- Notifications/reminders
- Dark mode theme
